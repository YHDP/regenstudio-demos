Triangle Playground

An interactive physics sandbox based on the Regen Studio homepage animation.

How to play

  • Creative Mode: Tune physics parameters, swap palettes, freeze and export your creation.
  • Constellation Builder: Build one cluster that covers all touchpoints to complete levels.
  • Cluster Rush: 60 seconds to form as many bonds as possible. Keep the combo going!

Controls

  • Hover / Touch: Attract nearby triangles
  • Click + Drag: Grab and move clusters
  • Click bonded cluster: Disperse it
