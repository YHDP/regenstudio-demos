### S2. EPBD Recast — Dual GWP Data Demand for Windows

**Source**: Directive (EU) 2024/1275 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 24 April 2024 on the energy performance of buildings (recast), OJ L 2024/1275
URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32024L1275
**Relevant articles**: Art. 7(2) — GWP calculation for new buildings; Art. 7(3) — thresholds; Annex III — methodology referencing EN 15978 / Level(s)

EPBD 2024/1275 creates a building-level GWP calculation requirement:
- **Large new buildings >2000m²**: GWP calculations required from **2027**
- **All new buildings**: From **2030**
- Building-level calculations pull in product-level CPR data via EN 15978 / Level(s) framework

Windows are a critical building component for both thermal performance (U-value) and embodied carbon (GWP). Manufacturers will face **dual data demand**: CPR GWP declaration in DoPC + EPBD building-level GWP contribution. National transposition (deadline 29 May 2026) will determine how member states implement these requirements.

This creates urgency for window manufacturers to prepare EPD/GWP data even before new CPR harmonised standards are published.
