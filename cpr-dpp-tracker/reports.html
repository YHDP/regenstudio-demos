What a product family profile contains

Each product family profile opens with a narrative regulatory context — the current CPR regime, expected transition to CPR 2024/3110, and sREQ status. This is followed by structured data tables and visual timelines.

Example: Standards table row

Standard Type TC AVCP OJ Cited Status

Following the standards tables, each family includes a milestone pipeline (6 stages from sREQ to DPP go-live) and a DPP pathway analysis showing the expected compliance timeline.

How we build these reports

Our analysis is grounded in primary EU regulatory sources:

  • Official Journal of the EU — harmonised standard citations and delegated acts
  • EOTA database — European Assessment Documents and Technical Assessment Bodies
  • CEN/TC work programmes — standard revision timelines and committee progress
  • European Commission sREQs — standardisation requests under CPR 2024/3110
  • CPR 2024/3110 text — transition provisions, delegated act framework, Annex IV

Reports are updated on a monthly cycle to incorporate new OJ publications, sREQ developments, and CEN/TC progress.
