What is VSME, and why should I bother?

VSME stands for Voluntary Standard for non-listed SMEs. The EU published it in July 2025 as a lightweight alternative to the full CSRD reporting regime. Think of it as a short, honest, one-page-per-topic way to tell banks, customers, and investors about your environmental + social footprint.

Technical note: Published by EFRAG under a mandate from the European Commission; adopted as a Commission Recommendation on 30 July 2025. Basic Module (~50 data points) and Comprehensive Module (~100) together cover the sustainability questions that counterparties like banks, procurement teams, and lenders most commonly ask SMEs.

What to do

  • Skim the plain-language glossary — you don't need to memorise the jargon.
  • Trust that this tool follows the standard; your job is to supply the data.
  • Remember: this is voluntary. You're doing it because it's useful.
