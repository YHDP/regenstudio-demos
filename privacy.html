Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 February 2025

Who we are

Regen Studio is a design and innovation studio. This privacy policy applies to the demos website at demos.regenstudio.space.

What data we collect

When you submit a contact form or demo access request, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your organization (if provided)
  • Your message (if provided)
  • The page URL you submitted from

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on this site.

How we use your data

  • To respond to your inquiry or access request
  • To send you a confirmation email
  • To notify our team of your submission

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making.

Where your data is stored

Your submissions are stored in a Supabase database hosted in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany). Confirmation emails are sent via Resend.

Who has access

Only the Regen Studio team has access to your submissions. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

How long we keep your data

We retain contact submissions for up to 12 months after the last interaction, after which they are deleted.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@regenstudio.world.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out to:
